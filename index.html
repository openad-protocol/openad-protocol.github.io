ABOUT US

xads is a novel ads network built specifically for Telegram Mini Apps. We are solving the current acute problem of the lacking ads monetisation tools for Telegram Mini Apps.

OUR TEAM

The project is led by Vadim Sterlin, the co-founder of Fanton, one of the largest games on TON, and industry professionals with 40 years of joint experience in the advertising market (including Yandex and other Big Tech companies)

OUR SUPPORTERS

TON foundation

We are happy to share that TON Foundation supported xads with a grant aimed to create a first version of the product and enable many game publishers from TON ecosystem to earn money on their traffic.
